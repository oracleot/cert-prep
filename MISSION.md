# Mission: Pass AWS Certified Developer - Associate (DVA-C02)

## Why
You are preparing for the AWS DVA-C02 exam and want teaching that turns the curriculum into fast, exam-ready recall and scenario judgment.

## Success looks like
- You can recognize which exam domain a scenario belongs to and choose the AWS service or feature being tested.
- You can answer scenario questions under time pressure by spotting keywords, constraints, and distractors.
- You can explain core DVA-C02 patterns for Lambda, API Gateway, DynamoDB, IAM, KMS, CI/CD, and observability.
- You can use hands-on labs to reinforce weak domains instead of passively watching review material.

## Constraints
- Keep lessons short, focused, and retrieval-heavy.
- Use `docs/aws dva-c02.md` as the local curriculum map.
- Prioritize official AWS documentation and AWS Skill Builder resources.
- Timebox topics: if a concept stalls, move to hands-on practice and revisit later.

## Out of scope
- Multi-certification study plans.
- Deep AWS architecture beyond what DVA-C02 tests.
- Passive summaries without practice questions.
